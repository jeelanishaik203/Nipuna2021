About our ISTE

A chapter of Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE), New Delhi was established on May 1st, 1995 in Sagi Rama Krishnam Raju Engineering College, Bhimavaram with the main motto of bridging the gap between the industry and the students. ISTE is always known for the quality of the outgoing creators who are enriched with acquaintance and skills. The organization has always backed the students by providing a platform to stabilize themselves as unique individuals. As a part of expanding student's managerial and technical skills, A National Level Techno-Management Symposium, NIPUNA was set into motion for 14 years. And till date, this symposium adheres to the standards of the organization. Encompassing a plethora of technical, non-technical and managerial events, NIPUNA strives to UNLEASH THE TALENT WITHIN.

Rules

1.A team should consist of a maximum 2 members.
2.Submission of abstract (in a word document)for not more than 300 words and in the form of IEEE format before the due date. Based on the peering review of the abstract, few papers will be selected for presenting the paper.
3.Only participants who qualify in the preliminary abstract submission round shall be eligible for the final presentation.
4.Violation of any rule can result in rejection of paper
5.On the event day the candidates should bring their respective college ID cards.
6.Qualified candidates are required to bring their presentation along with one soft copy(Preferably Pen drive) and 3 hard copies of the ABSTRACT on the day of the event.
7.The presentation should last a maximum of 6 minutes. There will be a 2 minutes query and answer session following it.
8.All the team members may be present during the event. There will be an interactive session with the judges, other participants and students during the time allotted for the event
9.The abstracts should be sent to specified E-MAIL ID below depending on your topic.
JUDGEMENT CRITERIA
The presentations will be judged by the judges of the event and their decision will be final in all cases.
